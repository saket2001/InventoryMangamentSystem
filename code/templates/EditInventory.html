Add product details to the inventory

Add all product details your inventory will contain. Don't add same product again to inventory.
For restocking products, make a invoice about that product first, and then update the product.But our smart feature will update the inventory once you make invoice so don't update the inventory yourself.

NOTE: This all settings are not required from you to perform. The inventory will be updated for you when you order the stocks via invoice.
Or when you sell certain products your inventory will be updated for you.

Update products in the inventory

You can simply update the inventory with the product which you want to restock to maximum limit.
Just add the product ID and click the button and inventory will reorder the product for you

You can also update the inventory with the product which you want to restock to certain amount. Just add the product ID and the quantity and click the button and inventory will reorder the product for you

We can update the inventory with the products which are close to their threshold limit .
Just click the button and inventory will reorder the products for you

You can update the product maximum limit your inventory can hold.
Just add the new limit and then click the button and limit will be updated.

Delete products from the inventory

Smart feature doesn't delete products from inventory on it's own. So remove any products from inventory if you want.

You can also clear the whole inventory . Just click the button and inventory will be cleared
